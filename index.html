How Scores Are Calculated

Score is based on net points for the day. Break even (net 0) = 2 stars. Rewards don't affect your score. Food rules: 5★ requires zero food spend. 4★+ requires zero bad food. Below 4★, only net points matter.

★★★★★ (5)Net +60 or more, no food spend at all.
4¾Net +60 or more, but any food was eaten.
4½Net +52, no bad food.
4¼Net +45, no bad food.
★★★★ (4)Net +35, no bad food.
3¾Net +28. (Bad food caps score here or below.)
3½Net +22.
3¼Net +15.
★★★ (3)Net +10.
2¾Net +5.
2½Net +1.
★★ (2)Net 0 — break even. The neutral baseline.
1¾Net −10 or better.
1½Net −20 or better.

★ (1)Net below −35, or nothing logged.

Food costs — Poor: A Bit −1, A Serving −5, Too Much −25. Bad: A Bit −5, A Serving −20, Too Much −40.
